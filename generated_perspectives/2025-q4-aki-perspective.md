## Re-think ... (optional)

**Here's something Aki may want to re-think:**

During sprint planning, I noticed Aki sometimes proposed technical solutions before we had fully understood the customer problem. I recognize that deadline pressure—especially when PMs need estimates quickly—pushes all of us toward familiar patterns, and Aki's deep technical expertise means solutions naturally come to mind fast.

**Here's an example to consider for doing it another way:**

**Exercise curiosity** by being the person who asks "What problem are we really solving for the customer?" before implementation discussions begin. Even a brief "5 Whys" exploration often surfaces constraints that make solutions more elegant. This small pause could strengthen already-excellent technical proposals.
