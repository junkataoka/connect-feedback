## Core Priorities — FY26

The 4 priorities Jun is being evaluated on this fiscal year (mirrors the Connect May 2026 Plan-for-the-Future goals).

### 1. Deliver high-impact AI/ML solutions that drive measurable business value
- **Why this:** Engagements only matter if they move a real business metric. Tailoring solutions to each client — meeting them where they are — is the core craft of the SR DS role.
- **Success signal:** Each engagement has (a) early-stage EDA that informs downstream choices, (b) model selection backed by rigorous experimentation, (c) repeatable evaluation tied to business impact, and (d) an ORA/RAI review whose recommendations are visibly reflected in the implementation. AI tooling (GitHub Copilot, Azure AI) used daily without compromising quality, security, or RAI rigor.

### 2. Deliver value through multi-disciplinary collaboration
- **Why this:** DS-only delivery is a bottleneck. The team's leverage comes from SEs, TPMs, and BPMs being able to engage meaningfully with AI/ML work — and from me partnering with them on production-readiness.
- **Success signal:** Measurable AI/ML contributions from non-DS team members; reduced DS dependency for routine ML tasks; positive cross-disciplinary peer feedback; early-stage feasibility shaping done *with* TPM/BPM rather than after the fact.

### 3. Treat knowledge as an asset and actively share it
- **Why this:** Hard-won engagement learnings die on the project if they aren't packaged for reuse. The DS Guild is the multiplier — and reusing existing assets respects the team's prior work.
- **Success signal:** Documented learnings and best-practice contributions to the DS Guild; presentations of engagement insights when warranted; demonstrable use of existing accelerators / shared artifacts before building new ones.

### 4. Compliance — generate and protect Microsoft trust
- **Why this:** Trust is the substrate everything else runs on. Living the Trust Code in daily decisions is table stakes for an SR role.
- **Success signal:** 100% on-time mandatory compliance trainings; integrity modeled day-to-day; proactive identification and timely escalation of role-inherent risks; cooperative engagement with audits and remediation.
